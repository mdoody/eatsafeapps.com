Privacy Policy

Eat Safe Japan — operated by EatSafe Apps
Last updated: 12 March 2026

1. Overview

Eat Safe Japan ("the App") helps travellers with food allergies navigate eating in Japan. Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

2. Data we collect

2.1 Data you provide

  • Allergen selections — stored locally on your device only. We never transmit your allergen profile to our servers.

2.2 Camera & image data

  • When you use the scan feature, your device camera captures an image of a menu or product label.
  • Images are processed using on-device OCR or sent to a third-party OCR service for text extraction. Images are processed in real time and are not stored on our servers.

2.3 Purchase data

  • In-app purchases are handled by the Google Play Store or Apple App Store and by RevenueCat, our subscription management provider.
  • We do not collect or store your payment information. RevenueCat receives an anonymous app user ID to manage entitlements. See RevenueCat's Privacy Policy.

2.4 Automatically collected data

  • We do not use analytics SDKs or tracking pixels.
  • Crash reports may be collected by the platform (Google or Apple) under their respective privacy policies.

3. How we use your data

  • To provide allergen checking functionality.
  • To manage your Pro subscription status.
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Data storage & security

Your allergen profile is stored exclusively on your device using local storage. No account creation is required. If you uninstall the App, this data is deleted.

5. Third-party services

The App uses the following third-party services that may process data on our behalf:

  • RevenueCat — subscription management
  • OCR.space / on-device ML Kit — optical character recognition for label scanning
  • Google Play / Apple App Store — app distribution and payment processing

6. Children's privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

7. Your rights

Since we store your data only on your device, you have full control. You can delete all data by resetting your profile in the App or uninstalling it. If you have questions, contact us.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top will reflect any changes.
