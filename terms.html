2. Description of service

Eat Safe Japan is a mobile application designed to help travellers with food allergies communicate their dietary needs and check food products while in Japan. The App provides:

  • A bilingual allergy card to show restaurant staff.
  • OCR scanning of menus and ingredient labels.
  • Pre-written phrases in Japanese and English.
  • Emergency information and contacts.

3. Important medical disclaimer

The App is an informational tool only and does not provide medical advice.

  • The App does not replace professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.
  • Always confirm allergen information directly with restaurant staff or product manufacturers.
  • In any medical emergency, call 119 (Japan) or your local emergency number immediately.
